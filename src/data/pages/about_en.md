Hi, I'm **Anas**, a hobbyist photographer who has always been drawn to visuals, design, and the quiet stories hidden inside everyday moments.

Photography came to me long before I ever held a camera. I grew up noticing light, colours, textures, and the way a scene can make you _feel_ something before you even understand why. Over time, that curiosity turned into a habit, and that habit slowly became a craft I wanted to nurture.

This portfolio is a small window into that journey.  
It's where I collect the moments that made me pause.  
The frames that felt honest.  
The scenes that reminded me why I love visuals in the first place.

I don't chase perfection.  
I chase feeling, the calm of a lakeside morning, the warmth of sunlight through trees, the quiet rhythm of Finnish nature, the simplicity of everyday life. My photography is my way of slowing down and paying attention.

Outside of photography, I explore design, storytelling, and digital creativity. These interests shape how I see the world and how I compose each frame. I believe visuals are more than images. They are experiences.
